Hum Saath Saath Hain is a 1999 Hindi language film directed and written by Sooraj Barjatya . It was produced by Rajshri Productions . The film stars Salman Khan , Saif Ali Khan , Mohnish Bahl , Tabu , Sonali Bendre and Karisma Kapoor in the lead roles, with Alok Nath , Reema Lagoo , Neelam Kothari and Mahesh Thakur in supporting roles. It became the highest-grossing film of 1999. [1]
